Adventures into woodworking

Woodworking started as a means to solve a problem - we needed a bedframe - and resulted in a hobby. With the help of a neighbor and some friends I was able to wrangle up enough tools to do my first project.

From there I never looked back and have continued to make simple furniture pieces with the help of free online tutorials. I enjoy the challenge, the logical work, the refinment process. Here I present some of my favorite pieces.
